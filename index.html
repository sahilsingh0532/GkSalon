Welcome to Gurukrupa Unisex Salon

At Gurukrupa Unisex Salon, we believe every individual deserves to look and feel their absolute best. Our salon is a sanctuary where style meets sophistication — a place crafted to offer premium grooming and beauty services for everyone, regardless of gender.

Founded and led by Shrikant Bidwe, a professionally trained stylist from the prestigious Alim Hakim Academy, Gurukrupa brings world-class techniques and a deep understanding of modern aesthetics to every appointment.

From precision haircuts to elaborate bridal makeovers, our team combines skill, creativity, and care to deliver results that exceed expectations.
